Master Thesis @ Classified Cycling

Improving End-of-Line Testing | Used in Giro d’Italia

In this project I developed a methodology for condition monitoring and multi-body dynamics modeling of gearboxes at Classified Cycling. The research aimed to improve reliability and reduce failures in high-performance cycling applications. The outcomes were successfully tested and implemented during professional races such as the Giro d’Italia.
